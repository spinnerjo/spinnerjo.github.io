Elipsicle
❮ ❯

To buy this mobile, return to Back and purchase it there. But please be prepared to wait for a couple of weeks while I make it.

The mobile is made with aluminium bars supported against a steel spine by a flexible sheath. The bar lengths are successive radii of an elipse separated vertically into a helix. The mobile is delivered flat, so you have to form the helix yourself - hang it up and twist it into shape. Anywhere betwen a half and a full turn looks good.

The Spinner is a quiet electric motor powered by 2 AAA batteries. Alkaline batteries last for about two months at slower speeds. It connects to the mobile through a variable length of nylon line. A timer chip delivers a pulse of twist to the nylon line every 90 seconds or so. By controlling the length of this pulse you can control the mobile spin rate from about 24 to 4 turns per minute.
